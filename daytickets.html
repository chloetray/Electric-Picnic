EARLY ENTRY PASSES

Early Entry ticket holders can enter the campsites from 4pm on Thursday 29th August. Ticket requirement details as below:

General campsites – you must have an Early Entry ticket + Valid Weekend Camping ticket to gain access. Pink Moon customers must have a Pink Moon Early Entry ticket + Electric Picnic Early Entry ticket along with their Valid Weekend Camping ticket and Pink Moon accommodation package.

Boutique customers must have the relevant Boutique Early Entry ticket + Electric Picnic Early Entry ticket along with their Valid Weekend Camping ticket and Boutique accommodation package. There will be no admittance to the above campsites on Thursday 29th August if you do not have an Early Entry Pass and a Weekend Camping Ticket (plus relevant additional tickets / accommodation packages for Pink Moon and Boutique customers).

Early Entry Passes are not valid for the Family Campsite or Eco Campsite, these campsites do not open until 9am on Friday 30th September.
