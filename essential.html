The Essentials

AGE POLICY

You must be aged 18 years or over to attend Electric Picnic Music and Arts Festival, with the exception of children aged 12 and under. As in previous years, 13-17 year olds are not permitted to attend the event even when accompanied by an adult.
It is not acceptable to turn up to the festival with children aged between 13-17 and anyone who is of this age will not gain admission to the festival.

ID

We have a Challenge 25 Policy on site and the following are accepted as ID for the bars and cigarettes. The accepted forms of ID are:
Passport
Drivers Licence
Garda Age Cards
Please note that ID checks will also be in place on entry to the festival. If you are bringing your child and feel they look older than 12 years old please bring ID to back this up (colour photocopies will suffice). It is also not acceptable to turn up to the festival with children aged between 13-17 years old. Children in this age range will not gain free admission to the festival or otherwise.

LOCKERS

Lockers and Storage Boxes will be available for storing your valuables all weekend in the Jimi Hendrix campsite. We recommend valuables such as cash, wallets, IDs, cameras etc are kept in lockers for extra security.
Storage boxes are perfect for storing larger items such as alcohol etc. If it fits in either, they'll will store it!
